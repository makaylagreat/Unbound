The Invitation
"She arrives as who she's been performing to be.
She leaves more connected to who she actually is."

Most women have spent years — decades — performing. Performing competence. Performing ease. Performing for partners, families, careers, and a culture that rewards self-erasure with acceptance.

Unbound is a weekend retreat for women who are done. Done shrinking. Done living on other people's terms. Women who sense there is a version of themselves beneath all the roles — and are ready to find her.

This is for the woman who —

  • Feels exhausted by the weight of being "on" for everyone else
  • Has been high-achieving but secretly feels disconnected from herself
  • Is in a season of transition — career, relationship, identity
  • Is drawn to somatic practice but hasn't had space to go deep
  • Is ready to stop optimizing and start inhabiting
  • Suspects there is more of herself waiting to be reclaimed
The Experience

The Modalities

Every element of the weekend serves a single arc: arrival → opening → descent → integration → emergence. These aren't activities. They're portals.

✦
Yoga

Grounding and arriving in the body. Each morning, a practice to call yourself back in.

◉
Somatic Breathwork

Releasing what is stored. The breath knows what the mind has learned to hold.

△
Ecstatic Dance

Liberating what's been contained. Movement without performance. The body as authority.

◇
Sensual Movement

Reawakening aliveness. Connection to the feminine body — not for anyone else. For you.

∿
Guided Hike

Body in the wild landscape of Southern Utah. Metaphor made real. The earth holds what words cannot.

○
Circle + Journaling

Witnessed presence. Making the invisible legible. Being seen without performing.
