A private home in the wild landscape of Southern Utah. Chosen for its quiet, its spaciousness, and its capacity to hold what needs to be held.

The Setting

A Home, Not a Hotel

Unbound does not meet at a resort. We gather in a private home, tucked into the red rock and sage of Southern Utah. The kind of place where the walls have already softened. Where quiet is the default, not the exception.

Each woman is given a bed in a space that feels more sanctuary than rental. Communal living areas become circle spaces. The kitchen becomes a gathering place. The land becomes part of the practice.

The space was chosen with as much care as the curriculum. Because the container matters.
